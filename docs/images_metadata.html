Image 3

Title

C. S. Rudolphi, Kristinehamn. Student at Kristinehamns Praktiskaskola 1917-18. Schoolmate of Signe Elvira Elgh born 1900.

Description

A studio head-and-shoulders-portrait of C.S. Rudolphi c. 1917-18 photographed by "Systrarna Hallberg Kristinehamn" wearing a blazer, bow tie and studentmössa 3/4 posed with a slight smile inserted into an embossed elliptical/arch-top-framed memory album pocket with the inscriptions "C.S Rudolphi" on photo; "Systrarna Hallberg Kristinehamn" printed on photo card; and "Kamrater från Kristinehams-Praktiska-skola 1917-1918" handwritten in ink below the photo on the album page.

Image 4

Title

Kristoffer Osterlund, Kristinehamn. Student at Kristinehamns Praktiskaskola 1917-18. Schoolmate of Signe Elvira Elgh b. 1900.

Description

An oval-framed studio head-and-shoulders-portrait of Kristoffer Osterlund c. 1917-18 wearing a blazer, bow tie 3/4 posed with hair parted to the side and a serious expression looking directly at the camera inserted into an embossed elliptical/arch-top-framed memory album pocket with the inscriptions "Kristoffer Osterlund" on the photo; "[not legible] foto" written on photo paper; and "Skolkamrat" handwritten in ink below the photo on the album page.
