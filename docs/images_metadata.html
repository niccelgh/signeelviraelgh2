Title

C. S. Rudolphi, Kristinehamn. Student at Kristinehamns Praktiskaskola 1917-18. Schoolmate of Signe Elvira Elgh born 1900.

Description

A studio head-and-shoulders-portrait of C.S. Rudolphi c. 1917-18 photographed by "Systrarna Hallberg Kristinehamn" wearing a blazer, bow tie and studentmössa 3/4 posed with a slight smile inserted into an embossed elliptical/arch-top-framed memory album pocket with the inscriptions "C.S Rudolphi" on photo; "Systrarna Hallberg Kristinehamn" printed on photo card; and "Kamrater från Kristinehams-Praktiska-skola 1917-1918" handwritten in ink below the photo on the album page.

Image 7

Title

Front cover of photo memory album "Kamrater från Kristinehams-Praktiska-skola 1917-1918" belonging to Signe Elvira Elgh (b. 1900 Molkom, Sweden)

Description

Four-pocket memory/photo album page with four photos inserted. Clockwise from top left: A front-facing head-and-shoulders studio portrait of Erik Friberg c. 1918 wearing a blazer, vest, bow tie, polka-dot pocket scarf and black studentmossa inserted into embossed elliptical-top-frame memory album pocket with the inscriptions "Erik Friberg" signed on photo and "1918" handwritten in ink on the photo and album page. A 3/4 length seated studio portrait of a classmate wearing a blazer, vest patterned tie with tie clip and white scarf tucked in blazer pocket and hair parted to the side 3/4 posed sitting in a wicker chair with hands clasped in lap, inserted into embossed elliptical-top frame memory album pocket. A 3/4 length seated studio portrait of F. Rexhammar wearing a white eyelet dress with a ruffle collar and locket, and whte gloves; and middle-parted hair gathered into a large white white bow 3/4 posed in a chair clasping a bouquet of daffodils, inserted into embossed elliptical-top frame memory album pocket with the inscription "F. Rexhammar" handwritten in ink on bottom of the album pocket. An full-length studio portrait of two people standing alongside one another posed directly at the camera. The person on the left is wearing a suit and tie with their hair parted to the side. The person on the right is wearing a pin-striped blouse with a large striped bow, two sets of buttons and a large collar, and a long skirt, inserted into embossed elliptical-top frame memory album pocket with the inscription "Syskonen Ekman Grythytran" handwritten in ink on the bottom of the album pocket.

Image 8

Title

Front cover of photo memory album "Kamrater från Kristinehams-Praktiska-skola 1917-1918" belonging to Signe Elvira Elgh (b. 1900 Molkom, Sweden)

Description

Four-pocket memory/photo album page with four photos inserted. Clockwise from top left: A head-and-shoulders front-facing studio portrait of a schoolmate smiling directly at camera with arms crossed on table wearing a dark blazer with a white shirt and black tie and hair parted to the side, with "Amanda Lundback Grythyttehed Filial: Loka" embossed in gold on photo card, inserted into an embossed elliptical-top frame memory album pocket. A head-and-shoulders oval-framed studio portrait of Bertil Cison Kanderyd 3/4 posed wearing a dark blazer with white scarf tucked into pocket and dark tie with hair parted to the side inserted into an embossed elliptical-top frame memory album pocket with the handwritten inscription Bertil Cison Kanderyd‚ on the bottom of the album pocket. A full-length studio portrait of Kusin Kalle posed directly at camera wearing a Swedish Army uniform c.1910 with side-parted hair, sitting in a chair with arms folded and legs crossed, inserted into an embossed elliptical-top frame memory album pocket with the handwritten inscription Kusin Kalle on the bottom of the album pocket. A head-and-shoulders studio portrait of Lisa posed directly at camera smiling wearing a white blouse with round pin with hair parted down the middle inserted into an embossed elliptical-top frame memory album pocket with the handwritten inscription Lisa on the bottom of the album pocket.
